Air Compressor

Completed: May 2023 – Jul 2023
Key Skills: Prototyping, Manufacturing, CAD Design, Project Planning

Project Overview

This project was a supplementary project to another invention I was working on. I needed a readily available source of compressed air, but I didn't have an air compressor at home. Instead of going and buying one from home depot, I elected to try and make a backpack-mounted version that I could hopefully use to carry compressed air or compressed fluid.

The body was made from two fire extinguishers that I purchased off of facebook marketplace, and the regulator fittings ensured that when pressurized, the assembly let off a consistent 100psi, while blowoff valves prevented the assembly from going over 150psi - the working limit I established for the two extinguishers.

Design Process

I tried to first design 3D printed fittings for the fire extinguishers, but after multiple failures, I elected to keep the factory-made heads and drill in my own access points for brass tubing.

The compressor unit came out of an old freezer unit we had at home. I took it apart, inspected the components, and salvaged the compressor. I briefly looked into a local AC repair company for industrially sized compressors, but I found none of them were easily portable so I left them behind.
